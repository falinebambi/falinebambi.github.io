State Library of Western Australian - Communications and Marketing

I worked in the Marketing and Communications team of the State Library in its 125th Anniversary year (a year that saw complete rebranding of the Library.) We acted as an in-house Design and Communications agency; overseeing all communications, branding, media and material for the seperate departments (clients) of the State Library. I wrote the Library newsletter and created a brand new design in celebration of the anniversary year, photographed and edited content for the Annual Report and assisted in design and editing of the Better Beginnings packs that are delivered to around 25,000 families a year. I also liaised closely with the media to promote the Library’s history collection and events.

    Western Australian Premier's Book Award

    In collabortaion with the Department of Culture & Arts the State Library runs the prestigious Western Australian Premier's Book Award. We project managed the 2014 cycle. The Awards are the key event in the State Library's calendar, attended by key local literary people and presented by the Premier. The organisation and management of the Awards cycle from judging process, branding, media, logistics, speech writing and planning the Awards night was in our scope.

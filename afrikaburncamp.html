    Afrikaburn Camp and Stage

    This is a noncommercial project very close to our hearts. In 2019 we organised, built and curated a large camp and stage at Afrikaburn (Second largest Burning Man Event) in line with the 10 Burning Man Principles. A total of 11 days in the remote desert, no reception, no money, nothing but what we bought to the playa, sand storms and wild conditions. 4 days of manually hard build of a 600sqm space and a Function One stage resulted in 5 days of curated workshops, ceremonies and world class DJs playing and musicians creating memorable experiences. free movement dance workshop with Dara Phitthayaphone DJ Line up - Vander Ruud Kalis
